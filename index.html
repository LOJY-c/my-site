Egyptian Chinese University • Project 2025

About the Project

A hybrid magnetic levitation prototype combining electromagnetic coils and permanent magnets for frictionless propulsion using IR sensors and high-power MOSFETs.

System Components

IRFZ44N MOSFET

High-current switching

Electromagnetic Coils

Dynamic field generation

Permanent Magnets

Track-embedded propulsion

IR Sensors

Precision position detection

Resistors

Circuit protection

Perfboard

Custom driver circuit

Working Principle

IR Sensor → MOSFET Activation → Coil Energization → Magnetic Propulsion → Contactless Motion

Applications

  • Advanced electromagnetic propulsion research
  • High-speed contactless transportation prototype
  • Educational platform for mechatronics and power electronics
